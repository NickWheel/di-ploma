About Us

Rinalogy was formed to a develop technology that quickly finds the most relevant information in Big Data. Founded by the partners who started RINA Systems and had successes in software for financial services the company’s focus is its Search and Classification products developed using machine learning.

Applying machine learning to recognize underlying relationships and learn from user feedback, Rinalogy’s patented methods let users train software models to perform classification and search with unparalleled speed and accuracy.

Our proprietary technology introduces new and significantly improved approaches to search and classification. This innovation positions our solutions as a disruptive force in the marketplace.

We help our users find the information they are looking for, quickly.
